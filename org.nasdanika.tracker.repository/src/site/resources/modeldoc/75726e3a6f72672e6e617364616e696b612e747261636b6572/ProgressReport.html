Progress reports are submitted by developers working on issues and are aggregated for issues/versions/projects. Progress reports are used in reporting. E.g. building a burndown chart for an increment or earned value/remaining effort calculation for a version and/or increment.

For progress reports it is recommended to have comments in the format:

  • Progress - what was done.
  • Plans - what is planned for the next reporting period (day/week)
  • Problems - impediments and risks which may need to be addressed.
